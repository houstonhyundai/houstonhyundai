Welcome to Houston Hyundai

Houston Hyundai Dealers is a Houston Hyundai Dealership located at 20440 Interstate 45 N #1, Spring, TX 77373. We sell Hyundai parts and accessories. We service and repair all Hyundai models. Our new car vehicle line-up includes: Hyundai Accent, Hyundai Azera, Hyundai Elantra, Hyundai Ioniq Hybrid, Hyundai Santa Fe, Hyundai Santa Fe Sport, Hyundai Sonata, Hyundai Sonata Plug-In Hybrid, Hyundai Tucson, Hyundai Veloster. We buy and sell quality used cars.

Most car shoppers are looking for a reliable, fuel-efficient new car, and Hyundai is the way to go. Our Hyundai dealership near Houston offers the best selection of new Hyundai cars and SUVs in Texas. Our inventory features every type of body style from sedans, coupes, SUVs and more. We even offer environmentally friendly options. With so many choices, you’re bound to find the perfect option for your lifestyle. If you’re on the fence or just don’t have a clue what you want, our friendly sales associates are more than happy to answer all of your questions and help you figure out which new or used Hyundai is right for you.

If you’re concerned about buying used, you may feel more comfortable with a certified pre-owned Hyundai. Feel free to ask our sales staff about the certification process. To become certified, our cars must pass a 150-point quality assurance inspection and have a clean, non-branded title. Plus, with a certified pre-owned Hyundai, you also get the remainder of the 5-Year / 60,000-Mile New Vehicle Limited Warranty* and 10-Year / Unlimited Mile Roadside Assistance*, reinstatement of the 10-Year / 100,000-Mile CPO Powertrain Limited Warranty**, a CARFAX® Vehicle History Report and more.
